➜WHAT IS INTERNET?

·THE INTERNET IS A GLOBAL NETWORK OF INTERCONNECTED COMPUTERS,SERVERS,PHONES AND SMART APPLIANCES.

➜WHAT IS CYBER SECURITY?

·CYBER SECURITY IS THE PRACTICE OF PROTECTING SYSTEMS,NETWORKS,PROGRAMS,DEVICES AND DATA FROM CYBER ATTACKS.

·CYBER ATTACKS ARE ATTACKS DONE BY HACKERS WHO ARE KNOWN AS BLACK-HAT HACKERS.

·CYBER SECURITY AIMS TO :-

1) REDUCE THE RISK OF CYBER ATTACKS.

2) PROTECTION AGAINST UNAUTHORIZED EXPLOITATION OF SYSTEMS,NETWORKS AND TECHNOLOGY.

3) PROTECTS US AGAINST PHISHING,RANSOMEWARE AND ETC.

➜WHAT ARE VIRUSES,MALWARES AND RANSOMEWARES?

·VIRUSES :- VIRUSES ARE A TYPE OF MALWARE THAT CAN SPREAD FROM DEVICE TO DEVICE. VIRUSES ARE DESIGNED TO DAMAGE A DEVICE OR STEAL DATA.

·MALWARES :- MALWARES ARE A TYPE OF MALACIOUS SOFTWARE THAT ARE DESIGNED TO INFILTRATE A DEVICE WITHOUT THE USER'S KNOWLEDGE. MALWARE CAN CAUSE DAMAGE TO A SYSTEM,STEAL DATA AND GAIN UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS TO A NETWORK.

·RANSOMEWARES :- RANSOMEWARE IS A TYPE OF MALWARE THAT BLOCKS ACCESS TO DATA OR A COMPUTER SYSTEM UNTIL THE VICTIM PAYS MONEY TO THE ATTACKER. RANSOMEWARE USUALLY ENCRYPTS THE DATA.

➜DIFFERENT WAYS OF CYBER ATTACKS

·SQL-INJECTION :- SQL-INJECTION IS A CODE INJECTION TECHNIQUE THAT DESTROYES A WEBSITES DATABASE.

·DNS-SPOOFING :- DNS-SPOOFING IS AN ATTACK INVOLVING MANIPULATING DNS RECORDS TO REDIRECT USERS TOWARD A FRAUDULENT, MALACIOUS WEBSITE THAT MAY RESEMBLE THE USER'S INTENDED DESTINATION.

·PHISHING :- PHISHING IS A CYBERCRIME THAT INVOLVES SENDING FRAUDULENT COMMUNICATIONS THAT APPEAR TO COME FROM A LEGITMATE SOURCE.

·DDOS (DISTRIBUTED DENIAL-OF-SERVICES) :- DDOS IS A TYPE OF NETWORK ATTACK WHERE CYBERCRIMINALS FLOOD NETWORK WITH SO MUCH MALACIOUS TRAFFIC THAT IT CAN'T OPERATE NORMALLY.

·MITM ATTACK (MAN-IN-THE-MIDDLE ATTACK) :- MITM ATTACK IS A CYBER ATTACK WHERE AN ATTACKER SECREATLY INTERCEPTS AND RELAYS MESSAGES BETWEEN TWO PARTIES WHO BELIEVE THEY ARE COMMNICATING DIRECTLY WITH EACH OTHER.

·XSS ATTACK (CROSS-SITE-SCRIPTING ATTACK):- XSS ATTACK IS A TYPE OF INJECTION ATTACK THAT INVOLVES INJECTING MALACIOUS SCRIPTS INTO TRUSTED WEBSITES.
